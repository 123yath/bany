About us

Banyan International School, Jammu, established under the aegis of the Shaurya Educational Society in 2008, is a co-educational English medium CBSE board school which provides quality education from Nursery to Grade XII. “Quest for Excellence” with our motto, and we strive to provide holistic development to the young leaders of tomorrow who will compassionately lead to change the world for the better. The school has a prodigious green campus spread over five and a half acres of land. The school envisions compassionate intelligent individuals in an atmosphere which enables them to think “out of the box”, question “conventional wisdom”, form their own opinions and belief, and respect diversity of thoughts. The democratic and secular atmosphere of Banyan International School enables children to develop mutual respect where there is no discrimination on the basis of caste, class, creed, gender or religious faith. The children here, learn to live in harmony with the environment, and among themselves for an inclusive, safer, resilient and sustainable future. Banyan International School along with its teachers aims to provide the children with ethical and educational foundation which will enable them to face the competitive world and inculcate the values of universal brotherhood.
